# Enviro-Governance Platform: B2B SaaS Business Plan

## Executive Summary

**Company:** Enviro-Governance Technologies  
**Product:** AI-driven Environmental Remediation & Multi-Stakeholder Governance Platform  
**Market:** Environmental Management, Water Treatment, Municipal Governance  
**Revenue Model:** SaaS subscription + Professional Services  
**Funding Ask:** $2.5M Seed Round  
**5-Year ARR Target:** $8-12M  

Enviro-Governance Platform solves a critical gap in environmental decision-making: **transparent, AI-powered multi-stakeholder consensus building for complex environmental policies**. Our platform combines biological remediation intelligence with blockchain-verifiable governance mechanisms, enabling municipalities, water treatment facilities, and environmental agencies to make faster, more credible decisions.
